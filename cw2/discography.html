POOLS TO BATHE IN

Pools to Bathe In is the debut extended play by the Japanese House, released on 27 April 2015 through Dirty Hit. The album was produced by The Japanese House alongside The 1975 band members Matty Healy and George Daniel.

available to stream here.

CLEAN

Clean is the 2nd extended play by the Japanese House, released on 6 November 2015 through Dirty Hit. Upon release, it received positive reviews from critics.

available to stream here.

SWIM AGAINST THE TIDE

Swim Against the Tide is the 3rd EP by the Japanese House, released on 11 November 2016 through Dirty Hit. The EP's first two songs were released as singles, and it peaked at number 12 on Billboard's Heatseekers album.

available to stream here.

SAW YOU IN A DREAM

Saw You in a Dream is the fourth extended play by the Japanese House, released on 30 June 2017 through Dirty Hit. Upon release, it received mostly positive reviews from critics.

available to stream here.

GOOD AT FALLING

Good at Falling is the debut studio album by the Japanese House. The album follows the entirety of a relationship. Through its 13 songs, Bain takes you from first introductions to the dizzying highs of love, onto the anxiety inducing pre-breakup and the crushing heartache of it all ending.

available to stream here.

CHEWING COTTON WOOL

The Chewing Cotton Wool EP was released on 12 August 2020. It featured four songs, one of which was a collaboration with Justin Vernon of indie folk band Bon Iver, as well as 2 previously unreleased singles. Upon its release, Chewing Cotton Wool received praise for showing further growth of Bain's signature lush, hazy production, introducing more textured and sporadic electronics.

available to stream here.
