Christian Bale

Christian Bale provides the voice for Howl Pendragon in the English version of the film.

Christian Charles Philip Bale (born 30 January 1974) is an English actor. Known for his versatility and intensive method acting, Bale is considered to be one of the finest actors of his generation. He is the recipient of many awards, including an Academy Award and two Golden Globes, and was featured in the Time 100 list of 2011.

Billy Crystal

Billy Crystal provides the voice for Calcifer in the English version of the film.

William Edward Crystal is an American actor, comedian, singer, writer, producer, director, and television host. He gained prominence in the 1970s and 1980s for television roles as Jodie Dallas on the ABC sitcom Soap and as a cast member and frequent host of Saturday Night Live.
